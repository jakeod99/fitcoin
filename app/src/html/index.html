What is Fitcoin?

Create a new competition by searching for an opponent's username, entering an amount of Ethereum to wager, and a date of the competition. Search Opponent Username: Ethereum to Wager: The ethereum amount will be removed from your wallet when the competition is created. If your opponent does not accept the competition, the ethereum will be returned to your wallet. Date of Competition: The competition will automatically be cancelled if your opponent does not accept your competition by the start date.
