Background

The Problem

Obesity epidemic in the US affects ~40% of adults in America, and many people have low incentives for walking.

The Idea

  • Make exercise social
  • Incorporate friendly competition
  • Introduce financial incentive
  • Take advantage of Ethereum's use of smart contracts

The Solution

What is Fitcoin?

The head-to-head exersize experience that utilizes cryptocurrency to facilitate a healthy competition between friends, coworkers, and anyone interested in wagering their walking!

Rules

Creating a Competition

Create a new competition by searching for an opponent's username, entering an amount of Ethereum to wager, and a date of the competition.

  • Opponent Username: Search for your opponent's username.
  • Ethereum to Wager: The ethereum amount will be removed from your wallet when the competition is created.
    • If your opponent does not accept the competition, the ethereum will be returned to your wallet.
  • Date of Competition: The competition will automatically be cancelled if your opponent does not accept your competition by the start date.
